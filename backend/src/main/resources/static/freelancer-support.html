Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid?
Once a client approves your milestone, the funds are released to your Nutzy Wallet. You can withdraw to your bank account or PayPal from the "Earnings" page.
How can I improve my proposal ranking?
Complete your profile 100%, verify your identity, and maintan a high job success score. Tailoring your cover letters to the specific job description also helps significantly.
What happens if a client doesn't respond?
If you have submitted work and the client is unresponsive for 14 days, the funds for that milestone will be automatically released to you.
How do I verify my identity?
Go to Settings > Identity Verification and upload a government-issued ID. This adds a "Verified" badge to your profile and increases client trust.
